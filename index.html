Accountancy, Business and Management Track

The Accountancy, Business, and Management track focuses on accounting, finance, business, and management principles. It involves analyzing financial information to aid decision-making in organizations.

Humanities and Social Sciences Track

The Humanities and Social Sciences track is a field of study that encompasses a broad range of subjects related to human society, culture, and behavior. They also develop skills in research, analysis, and interpretation of data, which can be applied to a range of career paths, including education, social services, law, journalism, and public policy.

Information and Communications Technology Track

The Information and Communications Technology (ICT) strand is a field of study that focuses on the use of technology to communicate, process, and manage information. It includes the study of computer hardware and software, programming languages, networking, database management, web development, and other related fields.
